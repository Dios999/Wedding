Venue Location

Information about our beautiful wedding venue and how to get there.

The Pavilion at Orchard Ridge Farms

6786 Yale Bridge Rd, Rockton, IL 61072

The Pavilion at Orchard Ridge Farms offers a picturesque setting for our wedding celebration. Nestled in the beautiful countryside of Northern Illinois, this elegant venue features stunning natural surroundings, a spacious reception area, and beautiful grounds for photos.

Transportation & Directions

  • Fly into Chicago O'Hare or Milwaukee Mitchell airports if coming from out of town.
  • Ample parking is available on-site.
  • Rideshare apps like Uber/Lyft are available in the area.

Directions from Chicago O'Hare International Airport

  1. Take I-90 W toward Rockford
  2. Continue on I-90 W for approximately 80 miles
  3. Take exit 1 for IL-75 toward South Beloit/Rockton
  4. Turn right onto IL-75 E/Willowbrook Rd
  5. Continue on IL-75 E for 3.5 miles
  6. Turn right onto Yale Bridge Rd
  7. The venue will be on your right after 0.7 miles

Directions from Milwaukee Mitchell International Airport

  1. Take I-94 W toward Milwaukee
  2. Continue on I-94 W for approximately 25 miles
  3. Take exit 328A to merge onto I-43 S toward Beloit
  4. Continue on I-43 S for approximately 45 miles
  5. Take exit 6 for I-90 W/I-39 S toward Chicago/Rockford
  6. Continue on I-90 W for approximately 10 miles
  7. Take exit 1 for IL-75 toward South Beloit/Rockton
  8. Turn right onto IL-75 E/Willowbrook Rd
  9. Continue on IL-75 E for 3.5 miles
  10. Turn right onto Yale Bridge Rd
  11. The venue will be on your right after 0.7 miles
